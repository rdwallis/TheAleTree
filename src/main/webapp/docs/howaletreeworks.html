How The Ale Tree Works

This is a simple guide to how the Ale Tree works.
Read on to find out how votes are counted and how content gets to the front page.

Basic Concepts

The principle idea behind the Ale Tree is that users are divided into teams before they vote, post links or comment.

The action is only performed if other members of their team agree with the action.

This stops collusion and spam because users can't choose their teammates.

Everyone's anonymous

Although we do log IP addresses to avoid duplicate votes, you'll never know who posted a link or comment. In this way content must survive on its own merits and is not affected by who posted it.

This also means that new users have exactly the same chance of getting a post to the front page as anyone else.
