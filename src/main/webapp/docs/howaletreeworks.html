How The Ale Tree Works

This is a simple guide to The Ale Tree.
Read on to find out how votes are counted and how content gets to the front page.

Basic Concepts

Before they vote, post or comment; users are divided into teams automatically by the server.

Their action is only performed if other members of the team approve it.

This stops collusion and spam because users can't choose their teammates.

Everyone's anonymous

Although we do log IP addresses to avoid duplicate votes, you'll never know who posted a link or comment. Content must survive on its own merits.

There's no reputation. New users have the same chance of getting a post to the front page as anyone else.
